Our Mission

The COVID-19 pandemic has greatly impacted the lives of people all around the world. Many people are unable to perform duties that are part of their daily lives. Senior citizens, people with compromised immune systems, and people with pre-existing medical conditions are at higher risk of contracting the virus and can put their health in jeopardy, just by leaving their homes. So, at Door Drop, we aim to support those who are at higher risk of being infected with COVID-19 by offering a free grocery delivery service. Customers will make their orders online, and will then receive their groceries at an agreed-upon pickup time with the delivery driver. This service will require no contact between the delivery driver and the customer to ensure the safety of our customers. There are many uncertainties during this time, so we strive to make people feel safe and comfortable. Thus, we will further aid the community by donating reusable masks to customers who utilize our service three times or more.

Steps to Order

Step One: Pick one of the compatible grocers.

Step Two: Choose a pick-up time that works for both you and us.

Step Three: Place an order with Instacart at a compatible time.

Step Four: Use Door Drop ( Your Name ) as the name for pickup.

Step Five: Fill out the order form by clicking on the “Order” tab located in the top right corner of this page.

Step Six: Your groceries will be delivered to your doorstep for you to enjoy!

*Note: If you experience any challenges in ordering online due to technological reasons, accomodations can be made by contacting the email or phone number above. We will be prioritizing orders for people who are aged 60+ or have immune disorders.
